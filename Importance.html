Importance of field training

1

It helps students to apply the theoretical subjects they acquired during the study period in a practical way, making them get the largest and widest area of understanding and understanding about their specializations. Provides an opportunity for trainees to integrate into the labour market.

2

Help students learn about the nature and needs of the labour market.

3

Give trainees an indication of their level and ability to assume work responsibilities.

4

Supports trainees and stimulates their creativity and innovation.

5

The trainee's field training gives a preliminary picture of the nature of the persons he will deal with after the training.
